Your Questions, Answered

How do I donate crypto?

Visit our Donate page, select a cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, USDT), scan the QR code, or copy the wallet address. Always verify the address before sending.

Why donate with cryptocurrency?

Crypto donations are secure, transparent, and have low fees. Blockchain ensures every transaction is traceable, so you know your funds are used as intended.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Donations may be tax-deductible depending on your country’s laws. We provide receipts for all donations; consult your tax advisor for details.

How is transparency ensured?

All donations are tracked on public blockchains. Check transactions using the explorer links on our Donate page (e.g., Etherscan, Solscan).

What projects do you fund?

We support open-source blockchain tools, humanitarian aid, and education. See our Impact Stories for examples.
